About the Creator

Dark Saga Art is a mystical universe of handmade tea companions and one-of-a-kind art dolls. Every piece begins with a character, a story, and a mood rather than commercial intent.

My process brings together the meditative calm of tea ceremonies, the earthiness of clay, urban romanticism, and the aesthetics of dark fairy tales. It is a search for a soul in the inanimate.
