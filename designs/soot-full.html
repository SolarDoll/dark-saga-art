The Maker

Everyone needs a friend on their own wavelength.

I can't find people for you — but I can make strange, magical companions who feel like home. Each one is sculpted by hand from clay and mixed media. No molds, no copies.

They can be arrogant, cute, odd or whimsical. But they're always real, and they never care about being normal.
